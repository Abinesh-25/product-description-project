Designs & Color

The design of the iPhone 14 Pro and iPhone 14 Pro Max is almost identical to older iPhone models, such as the iPhone 12 Pro and 13 Pro. The iPhone 14 Pro and iPhone 14 Pro Max have an new front camera called the Dynamic Island. The Dynamic Island can now bubble up alerts instead of having a notification. This new feature is on the new iPhone 15 series (iPhone 15, iPhone 15 Plus, iPhone 15 Pro, iPhone 15 Pro Max). The iPhone 14 Pro and 14 Pro Max are available in four colors: Silver, Space Black, Gold and Deep Purple. Deep Purple replaced the Sierra Blue color used on the iPhone 13 Pro and iPhone 13 Pro Max.

Pro Camera System

The iPhone 14 Pro is equipped with a cutting-edge triple-camera system. The flagship 48MP main sensor delivers stunningly detailed images with remarkable clarity, even in low light. Complementing this is a versatile 12MP ultra-wide lens, perfect for capturing expansive landscapes and tight spaces, and a 12MP telephoto lens offers impressive zoom capabilities without sacrificing detail. Enhanced with Night mode, Deep Fusion, and Smart HDR 4, the camera excels in diverse lighting conditions, producing vibrant, lifelike photos and videos. Additionally, ProRAW and ProRes video recording cater to professional needs, providing superior control and quality for creators. The system's advanced computational photography ensures consistently outstanding results, making the iPhone 14 Pro a powerful tool for both casual and serious photographers.

The iPhone 14 Pro combines cutting-edge technology with sleek design. It features a 6.1-inch Super Retina XDR display, offering vibrant colors and sharp details with a 120Hz ProMotion refresh rate for smooth scrolling. Powered by the A16 Bionic chip, it delivers exceptional performance and efficiency.
The camera system is robust, with a 48MP main sensor for detailed, high-resolution photos and advanced low-light capabilities. It also includes a 12MP ultra-wide lens for expansive shots and a 12MP telephoto lens with 3x optical zoom for close-ups. The front-facing 12MP TrueDepth camera supports Portrait mode and 4K Dolby Vision HDR recording.
Battery life supports up to 29 hours of talk time, with fast charging options including MagSafe. It comes in storage options ranging from 128GB to 1TB. The device is built with a surgical-grade stainless steel frame, a Ceramic Shield front cover, and a textured matte glass back, offering both durability and elegance. Running on iOS 16, it features 5G connectivity, Face ID, and MagSafe compatibility for easy attachment of accessories.
